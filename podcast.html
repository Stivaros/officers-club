Last Day Drama

May 2018

Stathi Stivaros, Tom Bodell, Jonathan Blakie and Will Evans discuss the only thing on every Barnet fan's mind - will the Bees avoid relegation from League Two on the final day of the season? We look back at the victory against Morecambe, ahead to the clash with Chesterfield, and chat about Saturday's all important game at the Ricoh Arena.

Climax

April 2018

With the season approaching a dramatic – and potentially dismal – climax, Stathi Stivaros, Will Evans, Jonathan Blakie and Tom Bodell get together to discuss defeat to Grimsby Town, grade Martin Allen's 4838493rd stint and insult the listeners. St Martin's Day

March 2018

Stathi Stivaros is joined by Will Evans, Tom Bodell and Jonathan Blakie to discuss the news that Martin Allen has returned to manage the Bees for a staggering fifth time. The panel chat about the appointment, dissect the short-lived Graham Westley era and answer your questions.
